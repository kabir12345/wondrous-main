We welcome you to the brand world of Wondrous. A world where creativity is nourished and good ideas are nurtured. We are not an organization but a creative culture where the feeling of 'JOY OF WORKING WHILE GROWING' is always focussed upon. Whether it is for our clients or for our employees.

We offer a host of services and we believe it is about having an integrated philosophy of creativity across everything that we offer.

We are an independent 360 degree advertising agency based in Udyog Vihar, Gurugram. Started by a Ekta, an advertising professional with an experience of 22+ years with top global advertising agencies of launching and building brands like ITC JOHNPLAYERS, TATA DOCOMO, Naukri.com, Jeevansaathi.com, Honda City, Dulux Paints, Whirlpool Home appliances, LG Electronics, makemytrip.com and many more. Born with a flair for fashion and luxury, Ekta has added sheen to many homegrown brands. Her poetry, song writing has added gravity and depth to many prominent brands.

Wondrous has worked on a bouquet of giant brands across all categories that enables it to accept the toughest challenges the communication business can offer. We thank you for visiting our website. We’re looking forward to a wondrous friendship with you and your very prestigious brand.
